ABOUT

Selfiltered is a final year BA Digital Media project that brings the experience of self presentation forward.

This installation invites you to enter the inner realm of who you really are when no one is watching in this age of digital technologies and constant surveillance- this installation aims to shift your curiosity to look beyond your devices as it poses the debate on self agency:

‘Are we in control of our self presentation or are we engulfed by the way platforms are designed’

- which in turn shapes the culture of self-presentation in social networking platforms. By raising these questions, we are able to conceptualise how young people are affected by the growing acceptance of this self-filtered norm.



THE INSTALLATION



Selfiltered is an immersive interactive installation that integrates augmented reality and face tracking technologies to redefine the experience of selfies in the age of social media. The installation allows participants to explore a dynamic virtual environment that responds and adapts to their facial expressions and movements in real-time, transforming their appearance.
